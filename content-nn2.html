About Us

Education is one of the most effective ways to break the cycle of poverty. Northern Kenya has always been one of the more poor and less educated parts of the country, standing in stark contrast to the more developed parts of the country in the south. As a result, World Vision has partnered with local teachers in Kenya to attempt to help with educational resources for young children in the north Kenyan region, including textbooks, school furnitures etc. Those equipments will be provided to students from a variety of villages in the surrounding area.

Goals:

Goal 1: Basic classroom equipments such as chalks, tables and desks ($400)
Goal 2: Elementary school textbooks for 60 students ($800)
Goal 3: Stationary and backpacks ($1200)
