Help Buy Meals for Children in Sudan

Food Bank Sudan is organizing a fundraiser to earn money to buy meals for impoverished children in the Sudanese area. Since the Sudanese Civil War, countless families and children have been displaced, many of them now living in poverty. Every summer, Food Bank Sudan goes to various villages in Sudan and conducts feedings, hoping to provide meals and food, particularly for children living in the area.

Help a School for a North Kenyan Village

Education is one of the most effective ways to break the cycle of poverty. Northern Kenya has always been one of the more poor and less educated parts of the country, standing in stark contrast to the more developed parts of the country in the south. As a result, World Vision has partnered with local teachers in Kenya to attempt to help with educational resources for young children in the north Kenyan region, including textbooks, school furnitures etc. Those equipments will be provided to students from a variety of villages in the surrounding area.

Nepal - Earthquake Relief and Recovery Fund

On April 25, 2015, a 7.8M earthquake struck Nepal near the capital of Kathmandu. On May 12, 2015 a second, 7.3M earthquake struck. Aid groups and local nonprofits are mobilizing to provide support and emergency supplies to the people impacted by both earthquakes.

Build a vegetable patch and small farm in an orphanage in Uganda

In Mpigi, Uganda, thousands of children are living without one or both parents, putting them at risk of dropping out, falling ill and being malnourished. In order to provide a safe place for the children, GlobalGiving has recently purchased two acres of land and built an orphanage.
