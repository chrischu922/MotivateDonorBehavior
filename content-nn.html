About Us

Food Bank Sudan is organizing a fundraiser to earn money to buy meals for impoverished children in the Sudanese area. Since the Sudanese Civil War, countless families and children have been displaced, many of them now living in poverty. Every summer, Food Bank Sudan goes to various villages in Sudan and conducts feedings, hoping to provide meals and food, particularly for children living in the area.
