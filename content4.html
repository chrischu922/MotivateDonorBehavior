About Us

In Mpigi, Uganda, thousands of children are living without one or both parents, putting them at risk of dropping out, falling ill and being malnourished. In order to provide a safe place for the children, GlobalGiving has recently purchased two acres of land and built an orphanage. In order to make the orphanage self-sustainable and to teach the children practical farming skills, so that the children will be able to provide for themselves once they leave the orphanage, we seek to make the additional land of the orphanage a vegetable patch and small farm.
